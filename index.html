Who we are

A long-term owner and builder, not a passive allocator.

19K Holdings is a principal investment and operating platform focused on cross-border market-entry opportunities in high-growth emerging markets. We commit our own capital and our own time — taking businesses from thesis to license to launch.

Our work spans digital financial services and energy and financial infrastructure, where regulatory complexity and cross-border structuring create durable advantages for operators willing to do the hard, patient work of building correctly.

We bring a fundamentals-driven approach to capital allocation, deep experience in investment evaluation and structuring, and the regulatory and multi-stakeholder fluency required to operate across jurisdictions.
